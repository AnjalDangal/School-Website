frequently asked questions

how to contact for help?

You can contact us via our contact form by filling it and submiting it. You can call us on provided number for queries and support. You can even send us email describing the issue on provided email. Thank You!

what is the best career in 2023?

We do not discriminate or humilate any career. But also we would like to suggest you to join computer engineering faculty for better future scope and career. We think computer engineering course as the best career in 2023. You can choose any course on your choice. Thank You!

how much fees for Computer Engineering?

As you all know the fees for technical education cost a little much than general stream. But looking at the cost of private institution our school provides the course in much cheap price. Basically, it starts Rs.11,000 per year from Class 9. It may increase gradually upto Class 12. Thank You!

can I choose my own tutor?

Sorry to say but its not available. But if you want a home tutor from our school you may get your own home tutor at suitable price. Thank You!

what payment methods are availabe?

You can pay us in different methods. You can visit our school manually for payment. If you can't reach school then you can pay us online via online digital wallets, mobile banking,etc. Thank You!

Do parents need to come to school while admition of student?

Yes, You must. Parent or Guardian must come to school with their child or children with required documents for admition. Thank You!
